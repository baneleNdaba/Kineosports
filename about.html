Founder: Geoff Botha

About Geoff

Geoff graduated with a BHSc in Human Movement Sciences and Sports Coaching (North-West University), majoring in Sport and Exercise Science (University of the Western Cape). With a background in High Performance & Strength & Conditioning, his true passion is Sports Recovery.

Having been an athlete himself, Geoff understands injuries and setbacks. He started Kineo Sport Science to help athletes increase performance and recover stronger with athlete-grade sports recovery equipment.
